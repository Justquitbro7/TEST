Steps to Screen Share in Lightstream or OBS

  1. Open Lightstream Studio: Go to studio.golightstream.com and sign into your account.
  2. Add Layer: Click the green + icon (Add Layer) next to the "Layers" tab.
  3. Select Screenshare: Choose "Screenshare" from the list of options.
  4. Configure Source: Select this browser tab to share.
  5. Enable Audio: Tick the "Share Audio" box to capture TTS.
  6. Adjust Layout: Resize the added layer in the preview window.
