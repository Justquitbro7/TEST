IMPORTANT: ENABLE AUDIO & TTS

To use screen sharing in Lightstream Studio or OBS, follow these steps to add a visual or audio layer from your computer:

  • Add the Layer: Click the green "Add Layer" button (the "+" icon) in your project's layer panel and select "Screenshare".
  • Select Content: A browser window will pop up. Choose whether to share your Entire Screen, a specific Window, or a single Browser Tab.
  • Enable Audio: If you want to include your computer's sound (e.g., for music or Discord), check the "Share Audio" box at the bottom of the sharing window before clicking "Share".
  • Customize: Once added, you can resize or reposition the screen share on your canvas. If you only need the audio, you can hide or delete the visual layer while keeping the audio source active in the Audio Mixer at the bottom left.
