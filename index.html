IMPORTANT! To enable audio functions and TTS:

To use screen sharing in Lightstream Studio or OBS follow these steps to add a visual or audio layer from your computer:

  • Add the Layer: Click the green "Add Layer" button (+) and select "Screenshare".
  • Select Content: Choose Entire Screen, Window, or Browser Tab.
  • Enable Audio: Check the "Share Audio" box at the bottom before clicking "Share".
  • Customize: If you only need audio, hide/delete the visual layer while keeping the audio active in the mixer.
