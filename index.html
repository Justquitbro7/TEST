To use screen sharing in Lightstream Studio or OBS follow these steps to add a visual or audio layer from your computer:

  • Add the Layer: Click the green "Add Layer" button (the "+" icon) and select "Screenshare".
  • Select Content: Choose whether to share your Entire Screen, a specific Window, or a single Browser Tab.
  • Enable Audio: Check the "Share Audio" box at the bottom of the sharing window before clicking "Share".
  • Customize: You can hide the visual layer while keeping the audio source active in the Audio Mixer at the bottom left.
