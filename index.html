IMPORTANT! ENABLE AUDIO & TTS

Follow these steps in Lightstream or OBS to share audio:

  • Click "Add Layer" (+) and select Screenshare.
  • Choose Entire Screen, Window, or Browser Tab.
  • Check the "Share Audio" box at the bottom before clicking Share.
  • If you only need audio, hide the visual layer while keeping the audio active in the mixer.
